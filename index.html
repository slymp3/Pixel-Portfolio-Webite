Every collectible features a distinct blend of backgrounds, colors, and items, making it a rare and coveted digital masterpiece. Powered by Bitcoin, RuneSprites evoke early 2000s video game nostalgia, appealing to RuneScape fans and digital art enthusiasts alike.

Why did RuneSprites use the Ordinals protocol?

RuneSprites utilizes the Bitcoin Ordinals protocol to store all data directly on the blockchain, making them fully self-contained. RuneSprites are also entirely immutable, meaning they cannot be changed in any way. This ensures the authenticity and permanence of RuneSprites by inheriting the security, immutability, and durability of Bitcoin.

Ordinal inscriptions are non-fungible, unique NFT-like tokens on the Bitcoin network that feature custom data, such as images, text, or video, inscribed onto them. Each satoshi is assigned a serial number based on the order in which it was mined, and these numbers are called ordinals.

The Ordinals protocol assigns extra information to the serial numbers of each satoshi, which is called an inscription. Ordinal inscriptions are embedded directly into individual satoshis and stored in Bitcoin blocks.
