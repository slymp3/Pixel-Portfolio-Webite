About RuneSprites

RuneSprites is a digital art collection comprising 2007 unique Ordinal NFTs inscribed on the Bitcoin blockchain. Inspired by the iconic game RuneScape, each RuneSprite reflects the nostalgic armor, items, and characters from its legacy.

Our mission is to immortalize RuneScape's essence through meticulously crafted sprite characters. Each RuneSprite is intricately woven into the Bitcoin blockchain, blending nostalgic charm with cutting-edge blockchain technology. This fusion offers collectors a timeless treasure with authenticity and permanence.

Every collectible features a distinct blend of backgrounds, colors, and items, making it a rare and coveted digital masterpiece. Powered by Bitcoin, RuneSprites evoke early 2000s video game nostalgia, appealing to RuneScape fans and digital art enthusiasts alike.

What are RuneSprites?

RuneSprites is a collection of RuneScape-inspired Ordinals inscriptions hosted on the Bitcoin blockchain. With a deep-rooted love for RuneScape, RuneSprites aims to immortalize its essence through meticulously crafted sprite characters. The collection contains 2007 randomly computer-generated RuneSprites.

Why did RuneSprites use the Bitcoin Ordinals protocol?

RuneSprites utilizes the Bitcoin Ordinals protocol to mint NFTs directly onto the Bitcoin blockchain, providing each satoshi with a unique identity. This protocol ensures the authenticity and permanence of RuneSprites by embedding them directly into the Bitcoin blockchain, enhancing marketability and desirability.

The Ordinals protocol assigns unique IDs to each satoshi, recording them on the Bitcoin blockchain to verify authenticity and ensure the correct Bitcoin ordinal series. This process makes RuneSprites immutable and resistant to modification.
