About RuneSprites

RuneSprites is a Bitcoin ordinals generative art collection inspired by the nostalgic characters, armor, and items from the game RuneScape. With a deep-rooted love for RuneScape, we wanted to show our appreciation by creating unique sprite characters that will live on the bitcoin blockchain forever. The collection contains 2007 randomly generated RuneSprites, each with its own individual combination of backgrounds, colours, and items, each with distinct rarity traits, that make every RuneSprite a one of a kind collectible digital art peice that will exist forever.
