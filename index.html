About RuneSprites

RuneSprites is an inovative digital art collection featuring 2007 unique Bitcoin-powered NFTs (non-fungible tokens) that are inscribed on the Bitcoin blockchain and feature procedurally generated art that encapsulates early 2000's videogame nostalgia, drawing inspiration from the beloved characters, armor, and items found in the iconic game RuneScape. Born out of a profound admiration for RuneScape's legacy, our mission is to immortalize its essence through meticulously crafted sprite characters. Each RuneSprite is a unique representation, intricately woven into the fabric of the Bitcoin blockchain. Each of the 2007 RuneSprites collectibles features its own distinct blend of backgrounds, colors, and items. Every RuneSprite possesses exclusive rarity traits, rendering each piece a rare and coveted digital masterpiece. With its fusion of nostalgic charm and blockchain technology, RuneSprites offers collectors a timeless treasure that will endure forever.
